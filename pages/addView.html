Add your data points in the dashboard

Follow the steps below to add your data sources

Choose the type of data point you would want to add for the respective source. Click 'Add to Dashboard'. Choose the authentication for the source. This will take you to the source website's login page. Authenticate over there and you'll be routed back to this page.

Now press the 'Add to dashboard' button to add the data-point to your dashboard.
